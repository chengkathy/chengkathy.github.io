metra


Print & Editorial Design

Metra is an art based magazine, focused on counter-cultural art primarily in Southern California. It is hand bound with original works including illustration, photography, found objects, and graphic art. Feature articles focus on underground comics, tattoo art, erotica, and graffiti. Metra was created to highlight the validity of counter-cultural art as true forms, standing against an art world that constantly upholds Eurocentric and western standards of art as it's only respectable standard.

Fall 2016

beerÿ app


Mobile UI/UX Design

The Beerÿ Mobile App was designed as an easier, more accessible way to discover and experience breweries. It was created with a platform for any beer drinking user - from the average, social drinker, to the well-versed beer connoisseur - looking to expand their horizons and digitally access local breweries. The app’s concept was designed with the idea of having a clean, functional, and laid-back interface that will attract and make the beer drinking experience all the more exciting for its users.
